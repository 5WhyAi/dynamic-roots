Child Support Transparency and Accountability

The current child support system lacks transparency and accountability, leaving many families in limbo. But technology offers a way forward. By using AI and digital payment systems, we can ensure accountability in child support spending while reducing emotional and financial strain on families.

Current Issues

  • 46% of custodial parents only receive partial payments.
  • 25% of custodial parents receive no child support.
  • Legal modifications often take more than 18 months to resolve.

Technology-Driven Solutions

  • AI-powered payment tracking to ensure funds are used appropriately.
  • Direct payments to child-specific debit cards for transparency.
  • Automated payment notifications and tracking for both parties.

Time for a Change

The current divorce process is fraught with inefficiencies that cause unnecessary delays and emotional strain. With technology, we can streamline this process, reduce the time families spend in court, and focus on what's importantâ€”moving forward with life.

Key Statistics

  • On average, first marriages last 7.8 years.
  • Divorces often last an additional 1-2 years.
  • Third-party interventions (e.g., parenting coordinators) can extend divorce processes to 5-7 years.

Technology Solutions for Divorce

  • Virtual courtrooms for faster hearings and decision-making.
  • AI to expedite paperwork, scheduling, and fee processing.
  • Automated mediation systems to reduce conflict and emotional manipulation.
