Key Takeaways

  • Out of 2 million annual marriages:
  • 1,180,000 (59%) are first marriages for both partners
  • 620,000 (31%) involve one partner remarrying
  • 200,000 (10%) involve both partners remarrying
  • Of the 820,000 marriages involving at least one remarriage:
  • 533,000 (65%) involve children from previous relationships
  • 287,000 (35%) do not involve children from previous relationships
